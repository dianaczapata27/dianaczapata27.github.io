Sigma Clipping

The foundation of this research is the SubQuaia catalogue.
SubQuaia is constructed from Quaia by applying two iterations of sigma-clipping in the plane of redshift \( z \) and its relative error \( z_{\mathrm{error}}/z \). After this filtering, we retain \(\simeq 67.2\%\) of the original sample with \( G < 20.0 \) and \(\simeq 61.3\%\) of the sample with \( G < 20.5 \).
